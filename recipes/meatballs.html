How to make Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

Sweet and sour meatballs, a delightful fusion of flavors, offer a quick and nostalgic culinary experience. Whether a childhood favorite, dorm room creation, or a simple pantry staple, these homemade delights, with succulent meatballs in a sweet and tangy sauce, provide a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Cup Dry Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt and Pepper, to Taste

Sweet and Sour Sauce

  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1/2 Cup Ketchup
  • 1/2 Cup Cider Vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Make meatballs: Mix beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well combined.
  2. Shape mixture into 1 1/2-inch diameter balls.
  3. Cook meatballs in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until evenly browned and cooked through.
  4. Cook meatballs in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until evenly browned and cooked through.
  5. Pour sauce over meatballs. Reduce heat to low and simmer until sauce is thick, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
